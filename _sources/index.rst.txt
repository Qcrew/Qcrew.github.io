

Qcrew documentation
======================

Introduction
-------------------

- `home page <https://quantumcrew.org/>`_

Welcome to the Quantum Circuits Research & Engineering Workgroup (qcrew)! 
We are a multi-disciplinary experimental research team in Singapore's 
`Centre for Quantum Technologies <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Centre_for_Quantum_Technologies>`_ . 
We build modular quantum devices to enable robust quantum computing and explore novel phenomena in quantum physics.
